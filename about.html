About us

Experiences are best shared with people you love! We are here to bridge the gaps in your story and connect your adventures to your friends and family in the fastest time possible. No longer do you have to retell a story and say “Well, you had to be there to find it funny”. Momento is here to make your stories shareable as soon as possible, with the people who care and from anywhere you’d like.
